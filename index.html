About Me

I am a doctoral researcher at Université de Technologie de Compiègne (UTC), LMAC Laboratory. My research integrates topology optimization and PDE analysis with AI-driven modeling for thermal energy systems, including phase change materials (PCM) and Kapitza interface laws.

Previously, I obtained my Master's degree in Computational Mathematics from Northwestern Polytechnical University (Double First-Class/985 University), under the supervision of Academician Weihong Zhang and Prof. Piotr Breitkopf. I received my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from the same institution.

🏆 Recent Achievement: Best Paper Award at ACCM-2023 (6th Australasian Congress on Computational Mechanics)

Research Projects

🔬Dynamic Multi-Fidelity Reduction Theory and Methods for Large-Scale Structural Dynamics Topology Optimization
National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) • Grant No. 12272302 • Jan 2023 - Dec 2026
Developing advanced reduced-order modeling techniques coupled with topology optimization for large-scale transient dynamic problems. Focuses on primal-dual POD methods and adaptive model updating strategies.
🌐Models, Theory and Methods for Scientific Computing
High-end Foreign Expert Introduction Program, Ministry of Science and Technology • Grant No. G2021183022L • Jan 2021 - Dec 2022
International collaboration project focusing on advanced computational methods and theoretical foundations for scientific computing applications.
⚡Large-Scale Stress-Constrained Topology Optimization Based on Reduced-Order Models
Natural Science Basic Research Program of Shaanxi Province • Grant No. 2021JM-043 • Jan 2021 - Dec 2022
Developing multi-fidelity dynamic reduced-order modeling strategies for stress-constrained topology optimization problems with millions of degrees of freedom.
🚀Integrated Design Methods for Functionally Graded Sandwich Structures for Lightweight Thermal Protection
National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) • Grant No. 11972166 • Jan 2019 - Dec 2022
Research on integrated design methodologies for functionally graded sandwich structures with applications in aerospace thermal protection systems.
